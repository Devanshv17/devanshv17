ReSTOX

An end-to-end real estate website with 3D views of properties and pin-point mapping of locations. It offers complete login functionality, including the ability to log in with Google. The website provides a comprehensive management system for listed properties, allowing users to effortlessly list and showcase their properties with detailed information and high-quality visuals. The platform's intuitive interface and advanced features enable seamless navigation, property search, and exploration, ensuring a user-friendly experience for both buyers and sellers.

Entrex

This app streamlines the entry-exit process at campus gates by leveraging QR code scanning technology. Students can register and log in using their iitk email addresses, ensuring secure authentication. Once logged in, the app's user-friendly interface displays a QR code scanner on the home screen. At the gates, students simply need to scan the designated refreshable QR codes to record their entry and exit times digitally. This efficient system eliminates the need for physical register books, reducing paper waste and promoting sustainability. With its simple and intuitive design, the app provides a hassle-free experience for students and security personnel alike, mitigating issues such as crowding and authentication problems.

EduWise

This web-based platform allows IITk students to securely upload and download course materials after logging in with exclusive permissions. Developed as a solo project, it facilitates sharing academic resources within the IITk community while preventing spam through robust authentication and security measures. The website promotes collaborative learning by enabling students to access a centralized repository of lecture notes, assignments, and reference materials shared by their peers.
